Cookie Policy

Introduction

Our online store specializing in PC and console games uses cookies to enhance your shopping experience. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and the choices available to you.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files stored on your device by your browser. They help us remember your preferences, enable core features of our site, and collect usage data to improve our service.

Types of Cookies We Use

1. Essential Cookies

These cookies are vital for our website to function properly. They enable features such as your shopping cart, secure login, and transaction processing.

2. Performance Cookies

These cookies collect anonymous data on how visitors interact with our game catalog and store. This helps us identify popular titles and improve your shopping experience.

3. Preference Cookies

These cookies save your preferred language, device type (PC or console), and other settings to personalize your visit to our game store.

4. Marketing Cookies

Marketing cookies help us deliver relevant promotions on PC and console games and measure the effectiveness of our campaigns.

How We Use Cookies

Cookies allow us to streamline your shopping process, analyze your interactions with our game listings, and provide targeted offers for new and upcoming PC and console games.

Managing Cookies

You can manage cookies through your browser settings by deleting or blocking them. Please note that disabling certain cookies may limit your ability to use some features in our game store.

Third-Party Cookies

Our store may use third-party services such as analytics and advertising partners that place cookies subject to their own privacy policies.

Policy Updates

We reserve the right to update this Cookie Policy as needed. Please review it periodically to stay informed about how we use cookies to improve your gaming shopping experience.

Contact Us

If you have questions about our cookie practices for this PC and console game store, feel free to reach out. We're here to assist you.
