Privacy Policy

Overview

Protecting your privacy and personal data is a top priority for our PC and console games online store. This privacy statement explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the safeguards we implement. By using our website and services, you agree to these practices.

Information We Collect

1. Personal Information

When purchasing PC or console games, we may collect personal details such as your full name, contact info, and any additional information you voluntarily provide during registration, checkout, or support inquiries.

2. Usage Data

We gather data about how you interact with our site, including your IP address, device type, browser, pages viewed, and time spent. This helps us optimize the shopping experience for gamers.

3. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our store uses cookies and similar tech to improve usability. These tools remember preferences, analyze traffic, and personalize your browsing. You can control cookies in your browser settings, though disabling them may limit functionality.

How We Use Your Data

1. Service Delivery

Your information enables us to process game purchases, respond to requests, and tailor your experience within our PC and console games platform.

2. Communication

We use your contact details to send updates, promotions, and relevant news about new game releases and deals. You may unsubscribe at any time.

3. Site Improvement

Analyzing usage data helps us refine our catalog, improve site navigation, and ensure smooth transactions for gamers.

4. Legal Compliance

We may disclose personal data when required by law or to protect our rights and interests.

Data Sharing

1. Trusted Partners

We may share data with service providers who assist in running our online game store. These partners are bound to protect your information under strict confidentiality.

2. Business Changes

In the event of a merger or sale, your data may transfer to the new owner, with your privacy rights fully upheld.

3. Legal Requests

We may disclose personal information when legally obligated to comply with judicial or governmental requests.

Security Measures

We implement advanced security protocols to protect your data. However, no system is fully secure, so please use strong passwords and share sensitive info only through secure channels.

Your Rights

1. Access and Correction

You can request details about your data we hold and correct any inaccuracies by contacting us.

2. Deletion

You may ask us to delete your personal data when it is no longer necessary or legally required to keep it.

3. Restriction of Processing

You can request us to limit how we use your data in certain situations, such as disputes over accuracy.

4. Data Portability

Upon request, we will provide your personal data in a structured, machine-readable format to facilitate transferring it to another provider.

5. Objection

You may object to our use of your data for marketing or legitimate interest purposes; we will honor your choice.

Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Please review it periodically for the latest information.

Contact Information

For questions or concerns about your data or this privacy statement, please get in touch with us. We're committed to supporting your gaming experience safely.
